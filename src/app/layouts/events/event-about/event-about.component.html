Welcome To Cork International Book Exhibition

The Cork international book fair organized by the Enterprise Ireland and Higher Education Ireland, it is an annual event held at the Pairc Ui Chaoimh and the Cork City Hall, selling and exhibiting books, printed matter, stationary, printing, compact discs and other multimedia publishing.

The Cork Book Fair is the global marketplace for rights negotiation and the sale and distribution of content across print, audio, TV, film and digital channels. In 2020 CBF will celebrate its 15-year anniversary. Staged annually, CBF sees more than 25,000 publishing professionals arrive in Cork, Ireland for the week of the Fair to learn, network, and kick off their year of business. The Cork Book Fair sits at the heart of Cork Book & Screen Week, which will take place from the 2-5 March 2020
